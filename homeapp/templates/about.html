Who We Are

Next-Gen Sea Logistics Platform

ShipNex is a modern digital logistics platform built to simplify and streamline sea-based shipping and container transport operations. We bring administrators, shipping companies, and customers together into one powerful system that handles ship management, container allocation, booking, route planning, payment processing, and customer feedback.

Our goal is to replace slow, manual logistics workflows with intelligent automation, real-time tracking, and transparent communication — ensuring faster decisions, reduced errors, and safer cargo movement.
